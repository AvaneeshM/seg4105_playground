Brainstrom:
  -	Understand the differences between the PM roles
  -	What are the different PM roles?
    o	Project Manager
    o	Product Manager
    o	Product Owner
   	
Research:
  -	Project Manager
    o	Is normally responsible for only one project
    o	Their goal is to make sure that deadlines are met
    o	Create roadmaps to give a clear idea of how the project is developed
    o	Success is measured depending on if deadlines were met and the quality of the project
  -	Product Manager
    o	Usually overlooks multiple projects that are related to the product
    o	Develops the product roadmap	
    o	Role is required throughout the entire lifecycle of the product
    o	Sets up product design and strategy
    o	Works on bringing the product into market
    o	Success is measure depending on how well the product releases and if it meets the clients need
  -	Product Owner
    o	Acts as a support to the Project and Product teams
    o	Is responsible for prioritizing the backlog and creating user stories
    o	They represent the stakeholders
    o	Gathers user requirements
    o	Reviews product roadmap with product manager
